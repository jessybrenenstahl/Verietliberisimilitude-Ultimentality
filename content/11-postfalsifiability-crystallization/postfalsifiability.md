# Postfalsifiability

## Definition

A **postfalsifiable** system is one whose current invariants are not axioms received intact but the **compressed residue of repeated, successful falsification of earlier forms**. Every load-bearing relation that now stands, stands because weaker or wrong versions of it were killed, and what survives carries the record of those defeats forward as present constraint rather than as ornament.

This is a third position, set against two neighbors it is easy to collapse into:

| Stance | What it does | Failure mode |
|---|---|---|
| **Unfalsifiable** | blocks defeat; no result is permitted to count against it | totalization — the claim is a shield |
| **Ordinarily falsifiable** | states tests and possible failures, once | passes a gate, then forgets it was ever exposed |
| **Postfalsifiable** | preserves the *causal consequences* of claims being killed, revised, retyped, or recognized as invariants | (see the kill condition) |

The difference between the second stance and the third is memory-with-teeth. An ordinarily falsifiable claim says *here is what would refute me.* A postfalsifiable claim says *here is what already refuted my earlier forms, here is the change that defeat forced, and here is what would refute me now.* The defeats are not kept as trophies; they are kept as [[negative-information|selected absence]] — prohibitions, dependencies, and regression tests that would fire again.

```text
attack → outcome ∈ {deletion, revision, retyping, invariant-recognition}
outcome → causal change (wording | mark | dependency | link | behavior)
survivors → compressed residue, each carrying a live kill condition
```

This is **one continuous iterative process, not a seed followed by a later realization**. Postfalsifiability is not the account of a rough origin that was afterward polished; it is a standing property of a correction architecture in which the same operation — encounter, outcome, compression — recurs on whatever currently stands. Reading it as two phases (a raw beginning, a mature arrival) both misdescribes the mechanism and smuggles in the chronology the framework refuses.

The **future-open condition** completes the definition:

```text
for every present invariant I:  kill(I) is live
```

No invariant is retired from jeopardy by having survived. Survival buys compression, not exemption.

## Type and formal status

**E (epistemic): Derived, CV.** "Postfalsifiable" is a carving — a three-way sorting of stances toward defeat — contestable by a counter-instance or a better carving. It is not FT: it does not follow from the bare definitions alone, and it is emphatically **not** a new founded, forced, or exempt tier. The invariants it describes remain **[[derived|Derived]]**; postfalsifiability is a claim *about how they earned their standing*, never a promotion of them out of contestability.

**A (alethic):** the page aspires to map an actual property of the correction process — that present structure is downstream of preserved, causally effective defeat. The aspiration is accuracy, not completeness: the "residue" story could be a flattering fiction, and saying so is an accuracy question, not an optional one. **Contestable never means probably wrong.**

This is a **treatise-side extension, held contestable** — never canonical, never a fixed point. It inherits the marks of the pages it leans on (**[[falsification-standard|the falsification standard]]**, **[[two-mark-system|the two-mark system]]**) and adds one CV relation of its own.

## What it regulates

Postfalsifiability regulates two opposite drifts in how a surviving framework describes itself.

Against **founded-invariant drift** — the [[textual-nephilim|Textual Nephilim]] move of quietly promoting a long-surviving relation to *given*, *forced*, or *self-evident* — it insists that every invariant is a survivor, and a survivor still carries the wound that could reopen. Longevity is not foundation.

Against **one-shot amnesia** — treating the framework as an ordinary hypothesis that passed its test and is now settled — it insists that the correction process does not close. A claim that has forgotten what it defeated cannot tell an [[negative-information|earned invariant]] from an untested assumption; both merely "haven't failed lately."

It therefore governs the [[falsification-standard|falsification standard]] downstream: it is why a surviving claim must stay reopenable, and why the record of prior defeat must remain causally load-bearing rather than decorative.

## What regulates it

Postfalsifiability is held in check by the apparatus that prevents it from becoming a survival-narrative.

- **[[causal-falsifiability|Causal falsifiability]]** supplies the teeth. A prior "falsification" counts only if it produced a change — in wording, mark, dependency, link, or behavior — with a reproducible trace. Absent that trace, the residue story is unearned, and the kill condition below fires.
- **[[outcomes-of-attack|The outcomes of attack]]** supply the discipline that confirmation is *not* an outcome. A failed attack is logged as a failed attack, never as evidence the survivor is true. This is the framework's standing prohibition: **never count a failed attack as confirmation.**
- **[[kill-table|The kill-table]]** and the future-open condition keep every present invariant's defeat condition live and named.
- **[[scar-record|The scar record]]** keeps the defeats inspectable, so that "compressed residue" refers to something exhibitable rather than merely asserted.
- **Recursive marking** — the [[semantic-closure-and-recursive-marking|closure–marking]] coupling — keeps *this* page Derived and CV, so postfalsifiability cannot exempt its own formulation.

## Property versus executable coverage

Postfalsifiability names a **property** and a **standard**, not a certificate of test coverage. Whether every current attack surface is *executable, causally wired, and regression-proven* is a **separate implementation claim**, and the two must not be read off each other:

```text
articulated kill condition     != runnable test
runnable test                  != causally effective update path
causally effective update path != truth certificate
```

A page can state a live kill condition (articulated) without that condition yet being a runnable fixture; a runnable fixture can pass without being wired to a governing update path; and even a causally effective update path certifies *correction*, never *truth*. Executable coverage is tracked in repository tooling and the [[attack-surface-matrix|attack-surface matrix]]'s regression column — not asserted here. A gap in coverage is a gap in **implementation**, not evidence that the property fails; and no amount of passing coverage promotes a surviving invariant out of [[derived|Derived]].

## Valid attack surface

Legitimate attacks target the carving or its earning, at its own level:

1. **Better carving.** Show the three-way distinction cuts no real joint — that "postfalsifiable" is ordinary falsifiability plus record-keeping, adding no distinct behavior. This is the [[attack-type-matching|counter-instance-or-better-carving]] attack proper to a CV claim.
2. **Unearned residue.** Take a specific present invariant and show its alleged prior falsifications produced no causal change — that nothing in the current structure would differ had the "defeated" forms never been attacked.
3. **Smuggled completeness.** Show that "compressed residue" is being used to imply the residue is *finished* — a completed distillation rather than a still-mortal survivor.

An invalid attack imports the wrong level: offering the *persistence* of the framework as proof of its truth, or offering a defeat at one abstraction level (a token, a surface expression) against a claim made at another. See the [[same-level-attack-rule|same-level attack rule]].

## What happens if isolated

Cut postfalsifiability loose from its regulators and it degrades in a characteristic way — and the degradation is symmetric, which is the tell that a coupling was doing real work.

```text
e_survival = survival read as confirmation (self-sealing shield)
e_amnesia  = kill conditions with no memory of what was defeated
e_survival regulated by → causal-falsifiability + the no-confirmation rule
e_amnesia  regulated by → preserved scar record + outcome classification
```

**Postfalsifiability without live kill conditions and causal teeth** becomes exactly the thing it was meant to exclude: a system that treats its own longevity as vindication — *attacked and still standing, therefore right.* Every failed attack is quietly reclassified as support; the [[self-sealing-test|self-sealing test]] fails; the [[textual-nephilim|Nephilim]] wins. Survival becomes a shield.

**The kill-condition apparatus without the postfalsifiability framing** becomes amnesiac one-shot falsifiability: a system that states current tests but has forgotten which alternatives it already killed and why. It cannot distinguish an earned invariant from a lucky assumption, and it loses the [[negative-information|negative information]] that made its present shape non-arbitrary.

## What larger property emerges from the coupling

Coupled to its regulators — defeat preserved *and* every survivor still killable — postfalsifiability yields **[[crystallization|crystallization]]**: a form that is at once **hard** (few surviving alternatives, tight type boundaries) and **open** (each survivor retains a live kill condition). Hardness without openness is a **fossil**; openness without hardness is mush. The coupling is precisely what separates a crystal from a fossil.

This instantiates, in the domain of a framework's self-defense, the same [[cross-regulated-necessity|cross-regulated]] pattern the framework asserts at its root — **[[no-escape-no-exemption|no escape, no exemption]]**. The local reading here is *no survival without live jeopardy, no jeopardy without preserved defeat.* Read structurally, postfalsifiability is [[vls-as-desire|VLS]] accumulated across repeated cycles of attempted destruction, correction, and continuation — desire-for-correction that has left a track record, not merely a stated wish. It is one selective pressure in the wider account of [[semantic-natural-selection|semantic natural selection]], and it inherits that page's warning: survival, popularity, and reproductive success are not truth.

## What would actually kill the claim

The claim that this framework is postfalsifiable dies in either of two ways:

- **No live kill remains.** Exhibit a present invariant for which *no* conceivable result is permitted to force deletion, revision, retyping, or demotion. If the update path is permanently closed, the invariant is founded, not surviving, and postfalsifiability is refuted where it is applied.
- **No causal change ever occurred.** Show that the alleged prior falsifications produced no causal change — no altered wording, mark, dependency, link, or behavior anywhere downstream. Then "compressed residue of survived falsification" is a story told over a fossil, and the [[causal-falsifiability|causal]] requirement is unmet.

A more radical kill: produce a **better carving** under which the three-stance distinction dissolves and adds no predictive or governing power beyond "keeps records." Postfalsifiability is a CV claim; a superior joint retires it.

Residue: this page cannot, by itself, prove that any *particular* invariant's residue was earned. It states the standard; the [[scar-record|scar record]] and [[kill-table|kill-table]] carry the case-by-case burden. Absent those, postfalsifiability is an aspiration, not a demonstrated property — and stating that plainly is part of leaving it answerable.

## Prohibited misreadings

- **"Post-" means past, above, or beyond falsification.** It does not. The prefix marks that falsification's *consequences are carried forward* as load-bearing residue — not that the system has graduated out of the reach of defeat. Reading "post" as "exempt" is the [[textual-nephilim|Textual Nephilim]] error compressed into one word.
- **Survived attack equals confirmed.** A failed attack is a failed attack. Never count it as evidence that the surviving claim is true; that is the self-sealing move [[outcomes-of-attack|the outcomes of attack]] explicitly forbids.
- **Postfalsifiable equals unfalsifiable with better manners.** The opposite: it *requires* a live kill condition on every invariant. A system that stops naming what would defeat it has left this category, however long its record.
- **A seed later realized.** Not a two-phase account of a rough origin and a mature arrival. It is one continuous iterative operation on whatever currently stands. This misreading also smuggles chronology, which the framework refuses.
- **Persistence equals truth.** Mere outlasting is not the point; the framework's telos is *answerable* continuation — [[symbolic-immortality|symbolic immortality]], meaning that stays answerable across the carrier's [[mortality-hevel|mortality]], never raw survival. A structure can persist by capture and be worthless.
- **The residue is finished.** "Compressed" is not "completed." The survivors are still [[derived|Derived]], still CV, still mortal formulations.
