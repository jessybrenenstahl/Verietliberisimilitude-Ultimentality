# Meaning as Weight

## Definition

A **semantic weight** is a retained relation by which one structured difference changes how another is subsequently handled — its salience, its probability of being selected, the gain applied to it, the threshold it must clear, or the path along which it is routed. Weight is the framework's name for **sedimented significance in its operative form**: not meaning held somewhere as a separate payload, but meaning as a standing disposition of a process to treat one difference differently in the presence of another.

```text
w(a,b,t) -> modulation of how a affects b at time t
```

Read this as a *disposition*, not a stored object. `w(a,b,t)` does not name a substance deposited in a carrier; it names the fact that, at time `t`, the presence or value of `a` conditions what `b` does downstream. The weight is not a hidden thing and does not have to be a number. Numeric parameters are one possible implementation among many — a synaptic efficacy, a price, the ordering of rules in a grammar, an institution's precedent, a branch in code, a settled habit — and each realizes the same relational fact: a retained difference that modulates future transformation.

The load-bearing move is directional. Meaning is **not** merely *carried by* weights as content that could in principle have been stored elsewhere. Where the retained relation itself determines what will count as significant next — where changing the weight changes which differences make a difference — the weighting relation **is** meaning in operation, not a container into which meaning has been placed. This is the page's identification, and it is what the attack surface below is aimed at.

## Type and formal status

**E (epistemic):** Derived, **CV**. This is a carving and a formalization — an identification of operative significance with retained modulating relations, together with a five-item list of modulation modes (salience, probability, gain, threshold, routing). Both the identification and the list are contestable by counter-instance or by a better carving that draws the boundary more accurately. Nothing here is FT: the frame is not compelled to individuate meaning this way, and a rival account that separates significance from disposition remains statable. Nothing here is AC: no controlling authority fixes the mode-list, which is a contestable count in the sense of the [[carving-tier|Carving tier]] and the [[two-mark-system|Two-Mark System]].

**A (alethic):** the claim aspires to *accuracy* about where meaning does its work — to name the operative locus of significance, not merely to license a permitted redescription. If the account is right, the picture of meaning as inert stored content is *inaccurate*, not just disfavored; if it is wrong, the error is a mapping error, correctable under the [[alethic-axis|alethic axis]] independently of the CV epistemic mark.

This page is a **treatise-side extension, held contestable**. It formalizes a relation that the directional and binding pages presuppose; it does not promote itself toward a forced or foundational status, and its formulations remain [[derived|Derived]] and mortal.

## What it regulates

The page regulates the excess of treating meaning as **detached content** — semantics conceived as a payload that a system stores and could, in principle, hold while making no operative difference. By defining meaning-in-operation as a modulating relation, it forecloses *inert meaning*: significance that alters nothing selectable, routable, persistent, or consequential. If a candidate meaning cannot be shown to bear on any future transformation, the page denies it the title of operative meaning and sends the question back to whether a weight is present at all.

Downstream, it supplies mechanism to three neighbors. It gives [[occurrent-and-sedimented-meaning|Occurrent and Sedimented Meaning]] the arrow from sediment to activation: sedimented meaning *is* the standing weights, and occurrent meaning is those weights discharged into a present transformation. It gives the [[semantic-transformer|semantic transformer]] one of the constraints it applies — weights are among the routing relations a transformation reshapes. And it disciplines [[emotion-as-regulated-binding|Emotion as Regulated Binding]] by locating the setpoint of a binding as a sedimented weight: the threshold against which a [[regulated-error-signal|regulated error signal]] is measured is itself a weight, so that a binding regulates *toward* a disposition rather than toward a free-floating fact.

## What regulates it

The claim is held in check by its own over-inclusion risk, and the regulators are the pages that keep a weight from being *any* causal relation. [[symbolic-is-not-linguistic|Symbolic Is Not Linguistic]] supplies the significance constraint: only differences that are processed *as* differences that matter, within a meaning-making system, are structured significance at all. [[meaning-maker|The Meaning-Maker]] supplies the boundary: a weight counts as *semantic* weight only where it sits inside a bounded recursive process that can admit, route, and be constrained by structure. Without these two regulators, every billiard-ball dependency — every case in which one state conditions another — would qualify, and the concept would name nothing.

The formalization is further regulated by the [[carving-tier|Carving tier]] and [[derived|Derived]]: the mode-list and the identification are marked, and a [[causal-error-mark|causal error mark]] on either must be able to change the wording, the list, or the claim's status. A weight-formulation that could not be moved by counter-instance would have exited the marking apparatus and forfeited its standing.

## Valid attack surface

There are exactly two openings, and each targets the identification directly rather than the packaging.

1. **Operatively inert significance.** Exhibit a *meaning* that alters no possible later selection, routing, persistence, or consequence — a difference that is genuinely significant within a meaning-making system yet bears on nothing downstream. Such a case would sever meaning from weight and refute the identification: it would show that meaning can be present where no modulating relation is.
2. **Significance-free weighting.** Exhibit a *weighting relation with no structured significance* — a retained modulating relation inside no meaning-making system that we would nonetheless be compelled to call meaning. This would show the identification runs the wrong direction: that weight is broader than meaning, and the two cannot be identified.

What does **not** touch the claim, per the [[selection-axis|selection]]/[[routing-axis|routing]]-level type of the target: pointing out that a particular weight is numeric or non-numeric (an implementation fact), that some weights operate below report (a delivery-layer fact), or that a given carrier realizes the relation unusually (a substrate fact). A valid attack must contest the modulation-plus-significance identification at the level of the operative relation, not at the carrier level.

## What happens if isolated

The definition is a coupling of two conjuncts, and either conjunct alone fails in a distinct, testable way.

- **Modulation without the significance constraint.** Drop the requirement that the difference be handled *as* one that matters, and keep only `w(a,b,t)`. The concept then collapses into *any causal influence whatsoever*: every retained relation by which one state conditions another becomes semantic weight, and meaning becomes coextensive with physics. This is precisely the kill condition realized from the inside.
- **The significance constraint without modulation.** Keep "structured significance" but drop the requirement that it bear on future transformation. Meaning becomes **inert content** again — a stored payload with no operative purchase — reintroducing the detached-content picture the page exists to prevent.

Neither half is the claim. A difference is semantic weight only when it *both* modulates a future transformation *and* does so within a system where the difference is processed as one that matters. The seam between the two conjuncts is where the page can be wounded, and naming it is what keeps the identification from drifting into either failure.

## What larger property emerges from the coupling

Coupling the modulation criterion to the significance constraint yields **operative meaning**: significance that is neither an inert stored substance nor undifferentiated causation, but a disposition inspectable *as* regulation. The gain is methodological as much as metaphysical — one can locate meaning in what changes what, without positing a hidden semantic substance behind the carrier and without dissolving meaning into every causal dependency.

Two further properties fall out. First, the sediment→activation→consequence→revised-sediment cycle of [[occurrent-and-sedimented-meaning|Occurrent and Sedimented Meaning]] becomes mechanizable: revision is just the updating of weights under returned consequence, continuous with the [[matter-meaning-cycle|matter–meaning cycle]]. Second, because meaning is located in the modulating relation (a type) rather than in the carrier (a substrate), the same weight-structure can be realized across different carriers without losing identity — which is the entry the page opens onto [[identity-across-substrates|Identity Across Substrates]].

## What would actually kill the claim

The claim dies if **the definition collapses meaning into any causal influence whatsoever** — if no retained causal relation can be exhibited that *fails* to count as semantic weight. A carving that cannot exclude is a carving that does no work; if the significance constraint cannot be made to exclude ordinary physical dependence, the page should be deleted or replaced by a better carving that draws the line where this one cannot.

It also dies to a successful run of either arm of the attack surface: a competent counter-instance of operatively inert meaning, or of significance-free weighting that we must nonetheless call meaning.

**Residue, left open.** The five modulation modes are a CV list, not a proof of exhaustiveness; a stronger carving might consolidate them (all reducible to routing, say) or extend them (a sixth mode not expressible as salience, probability, gain, threshold, or routing), and either would be a legitimate revision rather than a refutation of the identification. The line between "processed as a difference that matters" and "merely conditions another state" is the live boundary this page inherits from [[symbolic-is-not-linguistic|Symbolic Is Not Linguistic]] and does not itself close. That this page has so far excluded the inert-content and any-causation readings is logged as those attacks failing, not as confirmation that the carving is final.

## Prohibited misreadings

- **Weight as a hidden semantic substance stored in the carrier.** Prohibited. `w(a,b,t)` names a relation — a disposition to modulate — not a thing lodged in the medium. Nothing is "deposited."
- **Weight must be a number.** Prohibited. Numeric parameters are one implementation; the relational fact can be realized in efficacies, prices, rule orderings, precedents, or habits without any scalar.
- **Any causal influence is a semantic weight.** Prohibited — this is the kill condition, not the claim. Modulation alone is not enough; the difference must be handled as one that matters within a [[meaning-maker|meaning-maker]].
- **Meaning is the content, and weights merely carry it.** Prohibited inversion. Where the retained relation itself determines future significance, the relation *is* meaning in operation; treating it as a passive carrier reinstates the detached-content picture.
- **Weight is a primitive or a binding.** Prohibited. A semantic weight is not one of the four [[directional-primitives|directional primitives]] and is not itself a [[predicate-binding|predicate binding]]; it is the sedimented disposition — including the setpoint — *against which* a regulated error is measured. Weight is not the emotion, and it is not a conserved invariant.
- **Sedimented weight is inert storage.** Prohibited. Sediment is standing disposition, discharged into occurrent activation and revised by consequence; see [[occurrent-and-sedimented-meaning|Occurrent and Sedimented Meaning]].
